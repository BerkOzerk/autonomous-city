# Philosophical Sources for Autonomous Urban Theory

## Plato – The Republic
Virtuous order requires distributed power.

## Aristotle – Politics
The polis must be self-sufficient; excessive size destroys autonomy.

## Al-Farabi – The Virtuous City
Virtue must be embedded in the system, not individuals.

## Montesquieu – The Spirit of the Laws
Power balance prevents autocracy.

## Jane Jacobs
Mixed-use, walkability, diversity → resilient autonomy.

## Richard Sennett
Cities must align with human bodily behavior.

## Peter Barber – 100 Mile City
Resource cycles should be geographically local.

## Orwell – Animal Farm
The danger comes from internal power consolidation.
